About kabaddi

Kabaddi is a traditional contact sport that originated in ancient India and is popular across South Asia. It is played between two teams, with each team attempting to score points by raiding the opponent's territory and tagging as many defenders as possible without being tackled or running out of breath. The game requires agility, strength, and strategic thinking. A raider, chanting "kabaddi kabaddi," enters the opponent's half and must touch one or more defenders while holding their breath. If the raider successfully returns to their own half without being tackled, they earn points for their team. Defenders try to stop the raider by tackling or holding them, while the raider tries to escape their grasp. Kabaddi promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and quick decision-making skills. It is celebrated in tournaments and leagues, showcasing cultural pride and sporting excellence.

steps to play kabaddi

1. Form Teams:- Gather two teams of seven players each. One team will be the raiders, and the other will be the defenders.

2.Coin Toss:- Conduct a coin toss to determine which team will start as the raider.

3. Starting the Game:- The chosen team sends a raider into the opponent's half (court).

4.Raider's Turn:- The raider must enter the opponent's court and tag as many defenders as possible while continuously chanting "kabaddi, kabaddi" without taking a breath to indicate they are not inhaling.

5.Defender's Turn:- Defenders attempt to stop the raider by tackling them. The defenders must do this without crossing the center line into the raider's territory.

6. Scoring Points:- Points are scored when: The raider successfully touches a defender and returns to their own half. Defenders successfully prevent the raider from returning to their half.

7. Ending the Raid:- The raid ends when the raider either returns to their half or is tackled by the defenders.

8. Switch Roles:- After each raid, teams switch roles (raiding and defending) until all players from one team are eliminated or a predetermined time limit is reached. code
